Marketing Performance & Budget Optimization
This analysis provides a data-driven overview of marketing performance, identifying key channels for optimized investment and areas requiring attention.
Key Insights:
Top Performers: TikTok Ads (24.4x ROAS) and Influencer Marketing (23.4x ROAS) are our most efficient channels, delivering superior returns.
Underperformer: Email Marketing (5.4x ROAS) consistently shows the lowest return and a projected decline, signalling inefficiency.
Returns Impact: Product returns resulted in over $200,000 in lost profit, highlighting a significant area for cost reduction.
Budget Optimization: A reallocated budget of $600,000 to high-ROAS platforms is projected to generate $11.7 million in attributed revenue, a substantial uplift from current performance.

 Recommendations:
1.  Prioritize High-ROAS Channels: Increase investment in TikTok Ads and Influencer Marketing to maximize revenue growth.
2.  Re-evaluate Email Marketing: Significantly reduce or reallocate budget from Email Marketing due to its low and declining performance.
3.  Mitigate Returns: Implement strategies to reduce product returns, addressing both lost revenue and incurred costs.
